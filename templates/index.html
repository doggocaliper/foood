About

Are you a food business that faces food wastage when your food stock isn't sold out?
Are you needy for food that may not be available to you?
If you say yes to any of these questions, then you are at the right place!

How is the food wastage situation in Singapore?


Why "0 crumbs"?

"0 crumbs" is a webapp that helps the needy find unsold food that is available to them. Since the food would be wasted and go bad if it isn't sold, why not give them to the needy? This kills 2 birds with 1 stone, as it allows the needy to eat and prevents food wastage, leaving 0 crumbs.

How does this work?

As a food business or a beneficiary, you can look through "viewNeedy.html" or "viewFood.html" respectively to see who you want to work with.

From there, you can contact each other to form a partnership, and get unsold food delivered.

To advertise yourself, fill up the form depending on whether you are a Food Business or a Beneficiary (e.g. Charities).
